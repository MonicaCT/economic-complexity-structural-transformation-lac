# Limitations

- No internet, APIs, external downloads or bibliographic web verification were used in the final preparation phases.
- External literature references remain in `paper/references_to_verify.md` until full bibliographic metadata are checked from authoritative sources. They are not presented as verified BibTeX references and no DOI was invented.
- The analysis depends on local data availability and local source quality. Raw source folders and large processed caches are intentionally excluded from GitHub.
- The main product classification is HS92 at 4 digits. HS92 6-digit data exist locally but are much larger and reserved for follow-up detail.
- PCI is computed by product-side projection from the country ECI vector and should be interpreted as an internal projected product index.
- ECI is standardized annually; changes over time describe relative annual position, not absolute units of capability gained or lost.
- The Bolivia opportunity ranking is a screening exercise, not an investment recommendation or automatic sector-selection rule.
- The 2023 Product Space visual network is a legibility subset; density and Opportunity Gain use the analytical matrix.
- Fixed-effects models are observational associations and do not establish causal effects.
- The dashboard does not include a geospatial map because no public geospatial output is included in the repository; it uses trend, ranking, table and Product Space views instead.
- Phase 4 human review flags `8516` for a truncated local product label and `9303` as a sensitive regulated firearms category; both remain in the analytical base, but are excluded from general highlighted policy-facing lists.
