# Key Findings

- The processed panel covers 6,497,429 country-product-year observations, 242 countries, 1,243 HS92 four-digit products and 1995-2023.
- Bolivia 2023 has ECI = -1.236, diversity = 82, HHI = 0.124 and primary export share = 62.7 percent.
- Bolivia's HHI is slightly above the regional median reported in validation outputs, so concentration is interpreted comparatively rather than in isolation.
- The Product Space contains 701,978 analytical positive edges and a 698-edge visual subset; density calculations use the analytical matrix.
- The relative opportunity screen identifies 11 strategic bets and 239 incremental extensions, but these categories are not automatic policy recommendations.
- Fixed-effects models show descriptive associations between complexity and performance indicators; no causal identification is claimed.
