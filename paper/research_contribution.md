# Research Contribution

The contribution is empirical, interpretive and reproducible rather than methodological novelty. The project applies established economic-complexity concepts to a locally reconstructed Latin American panel and documents the full chain from data inventory to validation, figures, dashboard and paper.

Its Bolivia contribution is the dual opportunity classification. The strict absolute typology shows that close high-complexity quick wins are scarce. The relative typology then organizes Bolivia's constrained universe into feasible screening categories without changing validated rankings.

The regional contribution is to situate Bolivia among Latin American productive structures rather than treating it as an isolated case. The reproducibility contribution is the separation of raw data, processed caches, public samples, local paths and validation outputs.
